Title: Golgi-Associated Sterol Transport and Protein Binding System

Summary:
The system under analysis consists of 20 proteins that are involved in various cellular processes, with a majority of them being involved in protein binding, cytosol, Golgi apparatus, and membrane functions. The proteins are GORASP2, OSBP, TRAF3IP2, RNPEP, FAM174A, USO1, CCDC25, AGFG1, RAB1B, RFK, CD99, OSBPL11, UBQLN1, PPP5C, TPK1, QNG1, GGA1, SNX5, NUCB2, CWF19L1, and OSBPL9. The analysis aims to determine if this is a novel complex or if any proteins are novel members of a known complex. The system features several GO terms, including protein binding, cytosol, Golgi apparatus, membrane, intracellular membrane-bounded organelle, plasma membrane, nucleoplasm, cytoplasm, perinuclear region of cytoplasm, Golgi membrane, intracellular protein transport, endoplasmic reticulum membrane, sterol transporter activity, sterol binding, sterol transport, and others. The proposed name for the system is "Golgi-Associated Sterol Transport and Protein Binding System" to reflect its function and location.
