Title: Mitochondrial Small Ribosomal Subunit Complex with LRP2 and SMG5

Summary:
The system of proteins analyzed in this study consists of LRP2, MRPS10, MRPS14, MRPS15, MRPS2, MRPS21, MRPS26, MRPS27, MRPS34, MRPS5, and SMG5. The majority of these proteins (9 out of 11) are involved in mitochondrial translation, mitochondrial inner membrane, and mitochondrial small ribosomal subunit, suggesting that this system is primarily associated with the mitochondrial small ribosomal subunit complex. The two remaining proteins, LRP2 and SMG5, are involved in protein binding and may interact with the mitochondrial small ribosomal subunit complex. This system is involved in various biological processes, including translation, RNA binding, and cell population proliferation. The proposed name for this system is the "Mitochondrial Small Ribosomal Subunit Complex with LRP2 and SMG5."
