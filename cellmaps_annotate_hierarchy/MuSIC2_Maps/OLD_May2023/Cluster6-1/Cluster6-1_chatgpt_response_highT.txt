Title: Mitochondrial Ribosomal Complex with MTHFD2 and RHOT2

Summary:
The Mitochondrial Ribosomal Complex with MTHFD2 and RHOT2 is a novel complex consisting of proteins MRPL15, MRPL39, MRPL52, MTHFD2, and RHOT2. These proteins are localized to the mitochondrion and are involved in various cellular components and complexes, such as the mitochondrial large ribosomal subunit and the mitochondrial inner membrane. The primary mechanisms and biological processes performed by this system include mitochondrial translation, protein binding, and RNA binding. The inclusion of MTHFD2 and RHOT2 in this complex differentiates it from other known mitochondrial ribosomal complexes, making it a potentially novel complex worth further investigation.
