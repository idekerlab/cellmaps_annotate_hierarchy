Human Nuclear Spliceosome Complex

Summary


The human protein system analyzed consists of SNW1, BCAS2, CCDC12, CWC25, CWF19L2, PLRG1, PPIE, PRPF19, SNRPA1, SNRPB2, and SYF2. These proteins are primarily involved in mRNA splicing via the spliceosome, and are predominantly located in the nucleoplasm, nucleus, and nuclear speck. The system appears to be a part of the U2-type catalytic step 2 spliceosome and the Prp19 complex. Based on the provided information, this system can be referred to as the "Human Nuclear Spliceosome Complex."
